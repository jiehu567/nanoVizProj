What attributes associates with Rating?

The bar plot show how strong each of the attributes of a soccer player associates with overall rating in general. We can see: reaction, potential, age, short_passing, and long_passion are top 5 indicators for a high-rating soccer player. The height, however, performs pretty low correlation to overall ratings.

If mouseover the bar, we can see how the types of attributes distributed. (The types are defined by EA FIFA).

  • Personality attributes, like potential and age, are strongest indicator to overall rating
  • Power, mentality and skill attributes are more significant to indicate overall rating
  • Defending attributes locate at bottom of plot. They are less attractive
